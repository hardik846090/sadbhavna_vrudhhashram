Sadbhavna Vruddhashram is a leading charity in India working with and for disadvantaged elderly for nearly for 6 years. There are an estimated 106 million elderly in India. Sadbhavna Vruddhashram advocates for their needs such as shelter, food and warmth. It advocates for elder friendly policies and their implementation thereof. It works hand-in-hand with Senior Citizens Associations understanding elder needs working with and for them. The aim is to serve elder needs in a holistic manner, enabling them to live active, dignified and healthier lives. Sadbhavna Vruddhashram ’s focus over the years has moved from Welfare to Development. Providing free food and health services to elders we aim to let them feel secured, cared and loved. All donations to Sadbhavna Vruddhashram India are eligible for 50% tax exemption under section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

Manavseva trust began in early 2014 with one tree plantation and rest is truly history. The passion to reduce pollution and support farmers through planting trees continued to drive Manavseva trust towards mega projects in rural areas and mass tree plantations.

With the realization that innovation is needed in the plantation field to respond to the need for nature in our urbanizing cities, Manavseva trust started with few trees and today continues to plant lakhs of trees every year. Our work aims to connect people with nature in urban spaces through tree plantations and rejuvenating the ecosystem. Our focus is to fight the climate crisis and therefore we are working to provide sustainable solutions for a greener world through our projects Ecosystem Rejuvenation (Air, Water, Soil) and Sustainable Solutions. We continue to involve communities, raise awareness, and encourage implementation on the ground, ultimately working together to create a healthy and pollution-free Gujarat. We have planted more than 7 lacks trees over the years. Purchased 70 tractor and tankers to watering the pants and trees. 280 employees are working constantly to make this all happen. We opt for traditional trees such as Pipar, Ambali, Pipal, Neem etc which purifies air and gives endless health benefits as well.
